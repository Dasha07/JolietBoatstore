Shore Power

Our facility has 800 amp electrical power with 208/22 and 460/480 hook ups. If your boat requires extended periods of down time, we can offer large fuel and maintenance savings to you while your boat idles, hooking it up to shore power. This service not only reduces costs, it lessens your carbon footprint as well.
