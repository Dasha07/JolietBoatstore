Shore Power

If your boat has extended periods of down time, we can offer large fuel and maintenance savings to it while it sits idle and hooks up to shore power. Our facility has 800 amp electric power with 10 shore power hook ups in either 208/220 or 460/480, as well as a marine electrician on staff to assist with hooking up and checking rotation. On top of the savings, our shore power services also help your company lessen your carbon footprint.
