Waste

Waste is a very important part of the towing industry. Because of this, we offer safe, efficient, and environmentally sound solutions with proper documentation.

Trash

Household trash receptacles are located at our facility with easy access for your vessel.

Recycle

We urge all vessels to separate recyclables which we have a separate receptacle for.

  • Cardboard & Plastics
  • Electronics & Appliances
  • Lightbulbs
  • Batteries

Oily Waste

Oily waste needs to be separated with filters drained and then double bagged and taped shut. The filters are then crushed and recycled and the oil residue, if any, is put into our recycle program. Rags and paint please double bag and tape shut which will go out as special waste.

Bilges

All of our bilge and slop tank material go to proper and approved TSD facilities where the oil is recycled and the water is treated to all legal requirements.
