Groceries and Supplies

Groceries

Many boat store prices are less than desirable because of being marked up or covering huge overheads. This is why a gallon of milk can turn into a $10 purchase. We have a different approach, why not let the big chain grocery stores do what they do best with high volume and low costs?

With most major stores within a 10 minute drive of our river location, you can shop for yourself or we can do the shopping for you. The best part is that we will be passing on the savings benefits, the same we're all used to, right to the customer.
