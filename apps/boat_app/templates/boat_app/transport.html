Transportation

From Chicago to Hennepin, IL we can provide transportation for crew changes, airport runs, emergency pick-ups, grocery and supply deliveries, and anything else you may need. The towing industry is a 24/7 business and so are we, but the earlier our notice of your transportation needs, the more efficient our service can be. We are fully staffed, however, and are prepared for last-minute requests.

We firmly believe it is everyone’s responsibility to do their part in reducing their carbon footprint. That is why a diverse fleet is so important—we use the right vehicle for the job.

Our late-model vehicles are specific to each task at hand, from large vans for groups of people and heavier equipment to electric Volts that provide environmentally- friendly transport for smaller loads. Each manner of transportation is suited for efficiency and convenience.

Email dispatch@jolietboatstore.com for more information or to set up transportation services. For any other questions please call us at 844-JBS-TEAM.
